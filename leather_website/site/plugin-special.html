FAQ

I only need to build one website. Which plan should I get?

Elementor Pro empowers you to build complete WordPress websites.

The Essential plan includes all the capabilities required to build a basic website. You will get access to 50 Pro widgets, the Theme Builder to edit the header, footer, and other theme parts. This plan also includes basic marketing tools using the Form Builder, so you can stay connected with your website visitors.

The Advanced Solo plan includes 82 Pro widgets, and enables you to create ecommerce and professional websites. In addition to all the features available in the Essential plan, it also includes advanced marketing features like form integrations to integrate with your marketing software, the popup builder, ACF / CPT support for greater dynamic content support. This plan also includes ecommerce features with the full WooCommerce Builder, and integrations to PayPal and Stripe.

I want to build an online store. Which Shop plan is right for me?

Elementor offers three Shop Plans to match your business needs at every stage:

Shop Essential – Ideal for growing stores that need more design flexibility and customization. Includes Elementor Pro Essential (with 50+ Pro widgets, Theme Builder, and Form Builder), plus the Shoptimizer theme and the CommerceGurus kit. A solid option for stores looking to expand their presence and improve the shopping experience.

Shop Advanced – Designed for professional WooCommerce stores that want maximum performance and conversion power. Includes everything in the Advanced Solo Pro plan, plus additional advanced ecommerce capabilities, all supported by the Shoptimizer theme and the CommerceGurus kit for a fully optimized shopping experience.

Who is the Shop Advanced Plan for?

eCommerce professionals who want to build high-performing online stores with streamlined solutions that provide full design flexibility, optimized checkout experiences, and built-in sales boosters—without needing additional third-party plugins or custom development.

How is this different from the Advanced Solo Plan?

The Shop Advanced Plan includes everything in the Advanced Solo Plan, plus the Shoptimizer theme, which is specifically designed to enhance WooCommerce store performance, SEO, and conversions. While the Advanced Solo Plan provides powerful tools for building an online store, the Shop Advanced Plan goes further by offering a WooCommerce-optimized design with built-in conversion features like trust badges, distraction-free checkout, and performance enhancements—helping store owners maximize sales with less effort.

Can I switch to the Shop Plans from another Elementor Pro plan?

Currently, all Shop Plans are available only as standalone plans and cannot be upgraded to or from other Elementor Pro plans. If you need a different Elementor Pro plan, you can choose the one that best fits your needs directly from your Elementor account.

Can I upgrade from one Shop Plan to another?

Yes, you can upgrade from Shop Essential to Shop Advanced at any time from within your Elementor account. In your Elementor account, click “Upgrade Now,” select the plan you’d like to switch to, and immediately get access to the new features. Your Elementor account can grow with you, as there’s always a plan to fit your needs.

What is Shoptimizer?

Shoptimizer is a premium WooCommerce theme for WordPress, designed to improve store speed, SEO, and conversions. It enhances WooCommerce with performance-focused design elements and built-in optimization tools to help online stores drive more sales. The suite of conversion rate optimization tools, like order bumps and sticky “add-to-cart” bars, as well as behind-the-scenes performance optimization. You can learn more about it here.

What is WooCommerce?

WooCommerce is the leading open-source e-commerce plugin for WordPress, trusted by online stores worldwide. Its powerful features allow you to effortlessly add and manage products, integrate secure payment gateways, and set up shipping options to build your ideal online store.

How do I install the Shoptimizer theme after purchasing this plan?

After your purchase, you can instantly download and install the Shoptimizer theme on your WordPress website. Plus, you’ll always have access to re-download the file anytime from your account on my.elementor.com. For more details, follow the step-by-step guide.

Can I use this plan for multiple websites?

No, the Shop Plans are currently available for a single website per plan. If you need to build multiple eCommerce stores, you’ll need to purchase a separate plan for each site.

Do the Elementor Shop Plans include hosting?

The Shop Plans do not include hosting, however similar plans are available with Elementor Hosting. Learn more here.

What is the difference between the plans of Elementor Plugin and Elementor Host?

Elementor Plugin plans give you the Elementor Website Builder, available in a free version with core features and a Pro version that adds advanced tools like popup builder, custom CSS, form submissions, eCommerce optimization, and more. Pro is offered in multiple plans: Essential (basic websites), Advanced Solo (one professional or ecommerce site), Advanced (3 sites), Expert (25 sites), and Agency (1,000 sites).

Elementor Host is a complete, all-in-one solution that combines premium managed hosting with the Elementor Website Builder pre-installed. It is built for speed and reliability using Google Cloud Platform and Cloudflare Enterprise CDN, and includes a premium SSL certificate, plugin acceleration, unified support, and continuous performance optimization so you can build, launch, and manage your website under one service.

How can I change between the different Pro plans?

You can switch your Free or Pro plan at any time from within your Elementor account. In your Elementor account, click “Upgrade Now,” select the plan you’d like to switch to, and immediately get access to the new features. Your Elementor account can grow with you, as there’s always a plan to fit your needs.

Can I use my subscription on my subdomain?

Each subdomain requires a site allocation from your subscription.
Subdomains that are not considered as a license use:

  • dev.* (dev.example.com)
  • local.* (local.example.com)
  • test.* (test.example.com)
  • staging.* (staging.example.com)

See more information about using local and staging sites here.

Can I transfer the Elementor Pro license key from one domain to another?

Yes. You can transfer the Pro license key by deactivating Elementor from your previous domain and activate it on the new one. Manage your Pro license in your account via View Websites > Manage License. Switching it over is easy with no hassle or hidden fees.

Can I migrate my Elementor plugin based website to Elementor Host?

Of course. You can follow the following step-by-step migration guide to migrate your website, or contact our support to help you migrate your website to Elementor Host in no time, for free. During this migration process, your entire website will be transferred to a new Hosting subscription which is required before migration. It’s always recommended to backup and save a copy of your website for safety.

You can also use Elementor’s Import/ Export tool to export your existing website as a kit and import it to an Elementor hosted website. Please note that the tool has some limitations, which means you may be required to do additional work once the import process is complete.
